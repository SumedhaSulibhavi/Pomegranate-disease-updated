About Our Pomegranate Health System

Our Motive and Vision ‚ú®

Our primary motive is to support pomegranate farmers, agriculture students and researchers with an easy-to-use AI assistant for leaf disease detection. Pomegranate crops often suffer from fungal and bacterial infections that reduce yield and quality. By providing fast, image-based diagnosis directly from a mobile or laptop, our system helps users detect problems early, reduce unnecessary pesticide usage, and take the right action at the right time. Our long-term vision is to make plant-health monitoring affordable, accurate and accessible for every farmer, and to encourage data-driven, sustainable farming practices.

The Building Process üõ†Ô∏è

Building this Pomegranate Disease Detection System followed a clear, step-by-step pipeline. First, we prepared a diverse dataset of healthy and diseased pomegranate leaf images and carefully labeled each class. Next, we designed and trained a hybrid deep learning model that combines a CNN and Vision Transformer to capture both fine leaf textures and global patterns. We then connected this model to a Flask backend and created a clean, responsive frontend using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Tailwind. Finally, we tested the system with real images, optimized loading time and improved the user flow so that anyone can upload a leaf image and get disease prediction and precautions in just a few clicks.

Why Use Our App? üåü

Our application is designed to be simple for beginners and powerful for experts. It offers fast detection with high accuracy using a modern deep learning model, and it works with just a normal leaf photo taken from a phone or camera. Farmers can avoid guesswork and make informed decisions about spraying and crop care, which saves both time and money. The app also supports students and researchers by providing clear outputs, confidence scores and structured disease information. Because it runs on a web interface, it can be accessed anytime, anywhere, without installing heavy software ‚Äì making it a practical digital companion for pomegranate health monitoring.
